# 📊 Customer Segmentation & Sales Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing 6,000+ customers across 5 segments and 6 countries — built to help businesses identify high-value customers, retain at-risk ones, and understand revenue distribution.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
Businesses treat all customers the same — but not all customers are equal. This project segments customers to reveal **who drives revenue, who is at risk, and where to focus retention efforts.**

---


## 🔍 Key Insights
- 🏆 **Champions** generate ~$13M out of $17.37M total revenue
- ⚠️ A large portion of customers fall in the **At Risk** segment
- 🇬🇧 **United Kingdom** dominates with $14M in revenue
- 📉 Monthly revenue shows a **declining trend** — needs immediate attention
- 👥 Total of **6,000 customers** across 5 segments

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Launch **loyalty rewards** for Champion customers to retain them
- Create **win-back campaigns** for At Risk customers before they churn
- Focus growth efforts on **UK market** — highest revenue potential
- Investigate root cause of **declining revenue trend**

## 📁 Dataset Details
| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Customer ID | Unique customer identifier |
| Segment | Champions, Loyal, Frequent, At Risk, Others |
| Country | UK, EIRE, France, Germany, Netherlands, Australia |
| Revenue | Total revenue per customer |
| Order Value | Average order value per transaction |

## 📈 Dashboard Features

- ✅ Revenue Distribution by Segment (Bar Chart)
